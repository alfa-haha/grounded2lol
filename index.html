Grounded 2 FAQ - Start Smart, Survive Better

Get the insider knowledge you need about Grounded 2 to dominate the backyard from day one.

Is Grounded 2 currently available? +
Yes, Grounded 2 launched in Early Access on July 29, 2025, available on PC, Xbox, and through Game Pass.
Can I play solo or do I need friends? +
Grounded 2 supports both single-player and cooperative multiplayer for up to 4 players. The game is fully enjoyable in either mode.
Do I need to play the first Grounded? +
No, Grounded 2 features a fresh story beginning, making it accessible to newcomers while offering nods to returning players.
What platforms is Grounded 2 available on? +
Currently available on PC (Steam, Microsoft Store) and Xbox (Series X/S, One), with Game Pass inclusion. Steam Deck verified and ROG Ally compatible.
Does the game support accessibility features? +
Yes, including Arachnophobia Safe Mode, colorblind support, subtitle customization, narration through Read to Me, and various control options.
