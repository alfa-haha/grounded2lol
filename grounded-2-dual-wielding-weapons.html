Looking to dominate Grounded 2 with some seriously cool dual-wielding weapons? You're in for a treat. This comprehensive guide covers two of the most sought-after unique weapons in the game: the Ice Sickles and Northern Shredders. Both require special recipes hidden in ice and a whole lot of scorpion parts, so get ready to face some seriously dangerous stinging beasts.

Northern Shredders: The Easy-to-Miss Powerhouses

Finding the Hidden Recipe

The Northern Shredders are surprisingly easy to overlook, but they're absolute game-changers once you get your hands on them. You'll discover these during one of the early missions where you're tasked with checking out a tunnel and hunting for a stolen disc underneath the hot dog stand's decking.

Location Details:
  • Found in the tunnel beneath the hot dog stand
  • Guarded by cockroaches (they'll leave you alone if you don't attack first)
  • Expect to encounter scorpions, including a dangerous scorpion orc
Timing Strategy: Don't rush to this location too early in your Grounded 2 playthrough. Without proper gear, you'll get demolished by the enemies down there.

What Makes Northern Shredders Special

Once you find the chest with resources, keep your eyes peeled for a note containing the cryptic recipe for the Northern Shredders. As a tier 2 weapon, these bad boys are absolutely goated:

Combat Stats:
  • Massive critical damage output
  • Lightning-fast attack speed
  • Excellent stun capabilities (some of the best in their weapon class)
  • Critical hits deal additional venom damage

Required Materials:

  • Big scorpion parts (they're the only source for venom)
  • Caterpillar spine knobs
  • Other standard crafting materials

Ice Sickles: The Advanced Dual-Wielding Challenge

Preparation: Cold Weather Survival Essentials

The Ice Sickles are significantly more challenging to obtain and require serious preparation for the ice cart area. Here's what you'll need to survive the freezing temperatures:

Essential Gear Options

Option 1: Mighty Muffs (Recommended for Early Game)

  • Only requires the fuzzy helmet (tier 1, easy to obtain)
  • Much more accessible than full fuzzy armor sets
  • Can be combined with other warming items

Option 2: Full Fuzzy Parka Set (Advanced)

  • Requires extensive bee fuzz, milkweed tusk, and lingonberry
  • Loads of tier 2 resources needed
  • Bees are harder to find (hunt them at night when they're sleeping)
  • Honestly one of the toughest armor sets to craft early game

Warming Consumables: Your Lifeline

Blazing Brew Smoothies (Essential):
  • Reduce cold/freezing effects by approximately 50%
  • Key ingredients: spicy shards (easy with tier 2 hammer), lingonberry chunks (tier 2 axe required)
  • Add gum (tier 2 shovel needed) to extend duration
  • Apple bits for additional benefits

Beetle Bites Meal

  • Provides chill reduction
  • Requires bombardier beetle parts, toadstool chunks (from red mushrooms), and spicy shards
  • Combines perfectly with smoothies and mighty muffs

Budget Alternative: Spicy Chachars

  • Less effective than proper smoothies
  • Eat tons of them beforehand to unlock cold protection mutation
  • Not ideal but works in a pinch

Optimal Cold Protection Setup

Near-Perfect Protection (98% effectiveness):
  • Full fuzzy armor set
  • Beetle bites meal
  • Blazing brew smoothies
  • Cold protection mutation
  • Torch (adds about 1% protection)

Budget-Friendly Setup:

  • Mighty muffs helmet
  • Blazing brew smoothies
  • Beetle bites meal
  • Should be sufficient for most players

Navigating the Ice Cart

Multiple Entry Points

The ice cart offers several ways in:

  • Tunnel entrance: Directly underneath the cart
  • Side entrance: Around the right side facing south
  • Main entrance: Where the hatchway is open with ice cream bars visible
Pro Tip: Grab icicles from grass as you approach ‚Äì they work as drinkable water, and you'll get thirsty more often in the chilling area.

Combat Strategy for Scorpions

Recommended Weapons:

  • Smashing damage weapons work best
  • Cockroach hammer for stunning multiple enemies
  • Shield is highly recommended for defense
  • Special slashing weapons can work but aren't optimal

Enemy Density: Expect at least two major groups of scorpions plus scattered individuals throughout your journey.

The Ice Sickles Retrieval Process

Step 1: Reach the Mechanism Chamber

  • Follow the metal pipe leading to green ice cream area
  • Navigate through scorpion-infested passages
  • Look for the chamber with the restart mechanism
  • Watch out for baby scorpions in this area

Step 2: Activate the Mechanism

The mechanism isn't immediately obvious ‚Äì look for icicles covering it. Bash these icicles with your tier 2 hammer to get the mechanism moving and open the pathway to the main chamber.

Step 3: The Defense Mini-Game

Once you grab the Ice Sickles, you'll trigger a defense sequence against waves of scorpions:

Wave Composition:
  • Multiple waves with 2-3 scorpions each
  • Mix of junior and adult scorpions
  • Final wave includes a large scorpion

Combat Tips:

  • Fight is currently somewhat bugged (enemies spawn too far away)
  • You might need to actively engage some scorpions
  • Keep chilling effect below halfway to maintain attack speed
  • One blazing brew smoothie provides extended protection
Guard Dog Mutation: This defense sequence can activate the guard dog mutation if you haven't completed the mixer quest yet.

Claiming Your Rewards

What You Get

Unlike other special weapons (like the Cortana), the Ice Sickles give you both:

  • The weapon itself (immediately usable)
  • The crafting recipe for additional copies

The Buried Treasure

After completing the defense, explore the chamber thoroughly. Under the banana skins, you'll find buried treasure containing two mint globs ‚Äì essential for crafting additional Ice Sickles for your teammates.

Current Limitation: Since ovens aren't available yet in Grounded 2, these mint globs are the only way to craft extra Ice Sickles.

Getting the Treasure Key

The Lower Chamber

To access the buried treasure, you'll need a key from the lower area:

  1. Drop Down: From the main chamber (note: one-way trip unless you backtrack)
  2. Alternative Routes: Some ice cart entrances lead directly to this chamber
  3. Combat Strategy: Use arrows to cheese scorpions from above, targeting weak points like tails

Key Locations

First Body: Contains a chip that unlocks:

  • Fridge/freezer for food storage
  • New meal recipes
  • Milk molar finder functionality

Second Body: Located against pink ice cream, contains the actual treasure key

Ice Sickles Combat Performance

Weapon Stats and Characteristics

Strengths:
  • Excellent for critical hits
  • Very fast attack speed
  • Chilling attack effect (gradually freezes enemies)
  • Slashing damage type

Limitations:

  • Moderate stun capability
  • Not the highest raw damage output
  • Requires close-range combat
Build Synergy: Perfect for assassin/rogue builds, especially when focusing on mutation progression rather than unarmed combat.

Resource Requirements Summary

For Northern Shredders:

  • Big scorpion venom (from large scorpions only)
  • Caterpillar spine knobs
  • Standard crafting materials

For Ice Sickles:

  • Northern scorpion pincers and parts
  • Mint globs (limited availability until ovens return)
  • Cold protection gear and consumables
  • Tier 2 hammer for mechanism activation

Advanced Tips and Strategies

Scorpion Hunting Optimization

Since both weapons require extensive scorpion parts, develop an efficient hunting strategy:

  • Use smashing weapons for optimal damage
  • Always bring a shield for defense
  • Target weak points (especially tails) with ranged attacks
  • Stock up on healing items before major encounters

Team Play Considerations

Ice Sickles are particularly valuable for multiplayer since you get both the weapon and recipe immediately. Plan your ice cart expedition as a group effort ‚Äì the cold protection requirements and combat challenges make this ideal for coordinated team play.

Future-Proofing Your Gear

With oven functionality expected to return in future Grounded 2 updates, save those mint globs and start collecting materials now. The ability to craft unlimited Ice Sickles will be a game-changer for team equipment.

Both the Ice Sickles and Northern Shredders represent some of the most unique and powerful dual-wielding options available in Grounded 2. While they require significant preparation and combat skills to obtain, the payoff in terms of combat effectiveness and the sheer coolness factor makes them absolutely worth the effort.

The Northern Shredders offer an excellent introduction to unique weapon acquisition, while the Ice Sickles provide a comprehensive challenge that tests your survival skills, combat prowess, and resource management abilities. Master both, and you'll have some of the most coveted weapons in all of Grounded 2.
